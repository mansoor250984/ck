Concept of Operation

As a non-profit organization, The Colonel’s Kitchen will operate the 29 Diner as an ongoing fundraising enterprise in order to provide pro bono training for wounded warriors, disabled veterans and first responders, while maintaining flexibility to support “at-risk” community members and those that are food insecure.
